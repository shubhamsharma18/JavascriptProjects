Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive range of software services including custom software development, web applications, mobile apps, cloud solutions, and digital marketing services. Our team specializes in creating tailored solutions that meet your specific business needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A typical web application project can take 2-4 months, while larger enterprise solutions may take 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What technologies do you use?

We use the latest technologies including React, Angular, Node.js, Python, Java, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. Our tech stack is chosen based on project requirements and industry best practices.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your software continues to perform optimally. This includes regular updates, security patches, and technical support.
